SELECT 
    Name, 
    Salary,
    NTILE(4) OVER (ORDER BY Salary DESC) AS quartile
FROM Employee;

Example with 8 rows:
Name   | Salary | quartile
-------|--------|----------
Alice  | 100    | 1  ← Top quartile
Bob    | 95     | 1
Carol  | 90     | 2
Dan    | 85     | 2
Eve    | 80     | 3
Frank  | 75     | 3
Grace  | 70     | 4
Henry  | 65     | 4  ← Bottom quartile
