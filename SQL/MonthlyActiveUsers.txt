Problem: Monthly Active Users (MAU)
Description
Write a SQL query to calculate the number of Monthly Active Users (MAU) for each month. A user is considered active in a month if they performed at least one activity during that month.
Tables
Table: UserActivity
Column NameTypeDescriptionuser_idintID of the useractivity_datedateDate when the activity occurredactivity_typevarcharType of activity (e.g., 'login', 'purchase', 'click')
Sample Data

user_id	activity_date	activity_type
1	2024-01-15	login
2	2024-01-20	purchase
1	2024-01-25	click
3	2024-02-10	login
1	2024-02-12	purchase
2	2024-02-14	login
4	2024-02-20	click
1	2024-03-05	login
3	2024-03-10	purchase
5	2024-03-15	click
2	2024-03-20	login
4	2024-03-25	purchase


Explanation

January 2024: Users 1 and 2 were active (user 1 had 2 activities, but counted once)
February 2024: Users 1, 2, 3, and 4 were active

SELECT 
    DATE_FORMAT(activity_date, '%Y-%m') AS month,
    COUNT(DISTINCT user_id) AS active_users
FROM activity_table
GROUP BY DATE_FORMAT(activity_date, '%Y-%m')
ORDER BY month;
